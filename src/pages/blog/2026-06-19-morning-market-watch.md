## The Big Story: India IT in Freefall

The session's headline is brutal and clear — **NIFTY IT crashed 5.72%**, one of its sharpest single-day drops of 2026. The selling was broad and deep:

- **Infosys**: ₹1,035.70 — down **−8.14%**
- **Mphasis**: ₹2,195.10 — down **−6.06%**
- **TCS**: ₹2,070.50 — down **−6.03%**
- **HCL Tech**: ₹1,103.20 — down **−5.04%**
- **Persistent Systems**: ₹4,715 — down **−4.56%**
- **Wipro**: ₹176.15 — down **−3.66%**

The India VIX (fear gauge) jumped **+4.99%** to **13.30**, signalling that traders expect the volatility to continue.

## Money Flow: FII Selling, DII Buying

- **FII Net**: −₹1,025 Cr (foreign money exiting)
- **DII Net**: +₹3,517 Cr (domestic institutions absorbing the sell-off)

DIIs stepping in with ₹3,517 Cr is what kept the broader NIFTY from a steeper fall. But foreign re-pricing of Indian IT is the structural concern to watch.

## What to Watch Today

1. **FII flow continuation** — if selling persists over 3–5 sessions, IT won't recover quickly
2. **INR/USD** — a weak rupee historically provides a floor for IT exporters
3. **Management commentary** from TCS, Infosys — any revenue guidance updates will move stocks
4. **US corporate IT budgets** — the ultimate driver of Indian IT demand
